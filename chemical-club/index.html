~About Us~

The Association of College’s Chemical Engineering Student’s, known as “ACCESS” , is an apex student representation body formed under the department of Chemical Engineering with an objective of acknowledging maximum student participation in various intra-departmental events, such as, faculty-student interactions, departmental meetings,workshops, seminars on a diverse range of Hot Topics of current interest to the chemical engineering community. These events are organized periodically according to the event calendar throughout the academic year by the members of the association. Hence, developing sence of leadership & inculcating a sense of responsibility amongst the members of the association.It's an association of the Alumni, Faculty, Students and Staff of the Chemical Engineering Department.
